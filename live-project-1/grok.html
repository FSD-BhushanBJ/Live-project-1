About Us

Sai Transformation is a Katol-based metal fabrication company known for roofing sheds, metal and school furniture, gates, and window grills. A trusted branch of Sai Fabrication & Gas Welding (Est: 1998).

We offer a wide range of metal fabrication services, including gates, window grills, staircases, industrial sheds, roofing, and more. Our team handles projects from standard designs to unique, custom-built structures.
